Garden salad

A fresh garden salad with balsamic vinegarette

Ingredients:

  • 4 cups salad greens of your choosing
  • 2 cups mixed vegetables (sliced tomatoes, cucumbers, carrots, peppers, parsley, etc.)
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • Clove of garlic, minced
  • 3 tablespoons balsamic vinegar

Steps:

  1. Wash greens thoroughly, then dry.
  2. Add your vegetables/herbs of choice and toss with clean hands.
  3. To a small bowl, wisk together balsamic vinegar with minced garlic until well combined.
  4. While whisking, slowly add the olive oil until the dressing is emulsified.
  5. Combine dressing with salad and toss with clean hands, then serve.
