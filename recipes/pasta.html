Spaghetti aglio e oilo

Spaghetti with garlic and oil

Ingredients:

  • 1lb spaghetti
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • 6 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1/4 teaspoon red pepper flakes
  • 1/4 cup parsley, optional garnish
  • Salt, to taste

Steps

  1. To a boiling pot of water, add salt and spaghetti.
  2. Meanwhile, heat a large saucepan to medium heat, add olive oil.
  3. Add minced garlic and red pepper flakes, fry slowly until garlic is just golden, about 4 minutes. Remove from heat and set aside.
  4. Once pasta is fully cooked, using a spider or set of tongs, remove pasta directly from pot and add it to the saucepan.
  5. Stir is minced parsley. if using, and serve immediately.
